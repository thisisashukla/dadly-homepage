Support Policy

Dadly is built to help expecting and new dads navigate pregnancy and newborn life with more confidence. The following outlines how we handle support requests.

What we can help with

  • Technical issues — crashes, login problems, or features not working
  • Subscription and billing questions — charges, upgrades, and cancellations
  • Content concerns — inaccurate information or suggestions that feel off
  • Account and data requests — deleting your account or your data
  • General feedback and feature suggestions

Response time

We aim to respond to all support emails within 1–2 business days.

Subscriptions & refunds

Dadly offers a 7-day free trial followed by a $4.99/month subscription managed through the App Store. Subscription billing, cancellation, and refund requests are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. If you have trouble with this process, email us and we'll guide you through it.

Medical disclaimer

Dadly provides general information to help dads understand pregnancy and newborn development. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.
