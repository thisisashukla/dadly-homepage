Privacy Policy

Your data, handled with care.

This policy explains what Dadly collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: 3 May 2026

The short version

We collect only the information needed to give you week-by-week guidance and answer your questions. We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing dadlyapp@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

Dadly is a mobile app that helps expecting and new dads navigate pregnancy and the first year of fatherhood. This Privacy Policy applies to the Dadly iOS app and the website at dadly.app (collectively, the "Service"). For any privacy questions, contact us at dadlyapp@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide during onboarding

  • Whether you're expecting or already have a baby
  • Last menstrual period (LMP) date or your baby's birth date
  • Optional context — child's gender, country, and any pregnancy or recovery context you choose to share (e.g. high-risk pregnancy, previous loss, premature baby, C-section recovery)
  • Free-text notes you enter to personalise your briefs

You can choose to skip most of this. The minimum we need to function is your due date or your baby's birth date.

Account information

  • If you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique, anonymous Apple user ID and the email address you choose to share (which may be a private relay address).
  • If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account ID and email address.
  • We never see or store your Apple or Google password.

Conversations and content you submit

  • Questions you ask the in-app assistant and the answers it returns
  • Photos or PDFs of medical scans or reports that you choose to upload for explanation
  • Stories or thoughts you optionally share during onboarding

Subscription and purchase information

  • Subscription status, plan type, and trial state — managed through RevenueCat and the App Store. Apple handles all payment information; we never see your card details.

Technical and usage information

  • App version, device model, and operating system version
  • Crash and error logs that help us fix bugs
  • Anonymous product analytics — which screens you visited and which features you used — collected via Mixpanel on the website only

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photo library, or any health data from Apple Health.

3. How we use your information

  • To generate week-by-week briefings tailored to your stage
  • To answer the questions you ask the assistant and explain medical scans you upload
  • To save your context across devices when you're signed in
  • To process subscriptions and verify entitlements
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve the product
  • To respond to your support emails

4. Service providers we share data with

Dadly uses a small number of third-party services that process your data on our behalf. We share only what's necessary for each service to do its job.

  • Supabase — stores your profile (mode, due date / birth date, conditions, country, notes) and authenticates your sign-in session.
  • OpenAI / Anthropic — processes the questions you send to the assistant and the scans you upload, and returns explanations. Content sent to these providers is not used to train their models.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription state and verifies App Store receipts.
  • Apple App Store — handles all subscription billing, renewals, and cancellations.
  • Apple and Google — provide the sign-in flows you choose.
  • Mixpanel — anonymous website analytics only.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers.

5. Children's privacy

Dadly is intended for adults — expecting and new dads. The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, email dadlyapp@gmail.com and we will delete it.

6. Medical disclaimer

Dadly provides general information to help you understand pregnancy and newborn development. It is not a medical service, not a substitute for professional medical advice, and the explanations the assistant produces should never replace consultation with a qualified healthcare provider. In an emergency, call your local emergency number.

7. Data retention

We keep your profile information for as long as your account is active. Conversation history and uploaded scans are retained so you can refer back to them. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — see the next section.

8. Your rights and choices

  • Access and correction — you can view and edit most of your profile from the Profile tab in the app.
  • Account & data deletion — email dadlyapp@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
  • Cancel your subscription — manage or cancel anytime from your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Cancellations are handled by Apple.
  • EU / UK / California residents — you have additional rights under GDPR / CCPA, including the right to a copy of your data, the right to object to processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

9. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in services that use encryption at rest. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and notify you in the unlikely event of a breach that affects you.

10. International data transfers

Our service providers may process your data in the United States and other countries. By using Dadly, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in these locations.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use of the Service after a change means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact us

For any privacy question, data request, or concern, email dadlyapp@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.
